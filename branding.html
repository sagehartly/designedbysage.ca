Branding

Illustration, Typography, Branding

I re-branded this existing cupcake busines for a school assignment. I started by creating the logo using custom typography and bold colour choices. I wanted the logo to be fun, but professional and instantly visually interesting.

After completing a new logo, I carried the design through their business creating a cohesive and complete branding guide. I touched on how the brand should be carried through packaging, company stationary, merchandise, signage, social media and even to staff uniforms.
