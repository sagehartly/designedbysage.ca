Branding: Blue Sky School

Illustration, Typography, Branding

I recently had the pleasure of working with a new alternative school in Ottawa and developing their brand identity with their passionate staff.

My goal was to create a logo that showcased the importance of communication in this new type of school, as well as used the imagery of the blue sky to relate to the apt name!
