Book Cover

Typography, Knitting

This was an assignment to find a poorly designed book cover and come up with a design solution that better captured the essence of the book. The book I chose was a book of knitting patterns. To reflect the content, I actually created a pattern myself and knitted out the title.

The only digital elements are the subtitle and author's name. All knitting, layout and design were created by myself and captured in a photograph.
