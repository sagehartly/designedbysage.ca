I love to explore handlettering and am constantly practicing to improve this skill. When I'm relaxing at home, I'm often sketching in my notebook the lyrics to whatever music I'm listening to.

I lettered the piece on the left as the cover to musician, Jesse Dangerously's cassette tape and lyric zine. I also love writing messages on cakes at the bakery I work at, Little Jo Berry's. I also did a large handlettered piece on the back of a door at the bakery as well.
