Magazine Layout

Typography

A sample four-page magazine layout I created. I love doing layout and choosing type treatments for the context of a magazine. In every layout I create, I try to invoke the energy and feeling of the article's content.

This article is about low-budget, horror b-movies that were shared on VHS tapes so I wanted to recall the energy and gore of a slasher movie in the title type treatment. I thought that simple, bold graphic elements and a two-colour palette would work well for this piece.
